About us

Canberra Grammar School is a prestigeous school located in Canberra ACT. A few years ago it used to be a boys only school but it transfered to coed. You can start in the school from as early as preschool, at the age of four, and go straight up to Year 12. CGS is divided into different campus parts: ELC (Early Learning Center), Southside (Kidergarten to Year 2) Primary School (Years 3 to 6) and Senior School(Years 7 to 12). Canberra Grammar school also has many building located in campus, like a Library, Pool, Gym and May Ovals and Play Equitment. It also contains Basketball Courts, Amphitheatres, Chapels and much much more. It is a constantly developminng school and is largely environmetaly friendly. Down below is a map of the school.

A Day In the Life of Our of our students

So know we have covered the basics of what our school is and looks like, but in this paragraph we are going to be talkinng about what a students schedule looks like during the school hours.


In a normal student's schedule here, school normally starts at 8:30am when Juniour School has their Morning Meeting. In the Morning Meeting the Cohort teachers give a breifing of the day, any special events or fun facts that are cool to know, give birthday certificates and give shout-outs to any studennts who might have done something special. Then everyone goes inside ad the real day begins! After morning meeting the studets have their first period, which can be about a mixture of things listed in the paragraph right below this. For some classes this time can even be a specialist class time. (Again for more information see down below) This time period is from 9 to 10:00. After an hour the studennts have another 45 minute session of work untill 10:45 which again can be about any subject. Finally at 10:45, they have their first break, in which they sit with their friends in the eatinng are for their cohort and eat lunch untill 11. At 11 the eatign bell rings which signals that it is time for lunch and the studets make their way to the playground. Some students who want to, stay in this time at the eating area and either play there or just keep talking and eating with their friends. At 11:30 the end of break bell rings and the people in the playground make their way back up to the classrooms.Then from 11:30 to 12:15 students have another sessio of work, and then another untill 1. At 1 they have their 2nd Break, which is pretty much the same thing as first break but only for 30 minutes. At 1:30 again the bell rings and the students get ready for another 2 sessions-1:30 to 2:15 and 2:15 to 3 before... HOME TIME! at 3:20 (students spend the time from 3 to 3:20 packing up) they get to get in their parents car and drive home. For those students whose parents can't pick them up in that time frame, they get to go to an afterschool activity. This Activities include: Taekwondo, Cooking, Chess, Art, Debating, Coding, Engineering, Lego Robotics, Acedemic Club and much much more. Students can also come to the spacios library!
